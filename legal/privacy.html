Privacy Policy – OnHit

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how OnHit (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and stores data when you use the OnHit mobile application (“App”).

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate the App:

  • Displayed Name – used as a unique identifier within the App
  • Hashed Email – stored in a non-reversible form for identification purposes
  • Availability Data – selected date, time, and locations
  • Messages – chat messages exchanged between users
  • Technical Data – including push notification tokens, access tokens, and refresh tokens (JWT)

We do not store plain email addresses in our database.

3. Use of Email

Email addresses may be used temporarily (e.g. for password reset), but are not stored in our database in plain form.

4. How We Use Data

We use collected data to:

  • operate and maintain the App,
  • enable user matching,
  • facilitate communication between users,
  • send push notifications,
  • ensure security and prevent abuse.

5. Push Notifications

The App uses push notifications to inform users about matches and activity. This requires storing device-related tokens.

6. Data Storage

Data is stored using:

  • Microsoft Azure infrastructure located in Switzerland,
  • Firebase and Google services for push notifications.

Data may be processed on servers operated by these providers.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Limited data (such as Displayed Name and availability) is visible to other users as part of the App’s functionality.

8. Data Retention

  • Chat messages may be deleted after one (1) month without notice.
  • Other data is stored as long as the account is active.
  • Account deletion may take up to 14 days.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect data. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. User Rights

Users may request account deletion at any time.

11. No GPS Tracking

The App does not use GPS tracking. Locations are selected manually from a predefined list.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated version.
