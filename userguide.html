After confirming your preferences, the main app screen will appear.

Main App Overview

Overview of the main application screens

Main screen

Here you can see hour tiles. Abouve the hour you can see 2 gray bars representing your 2 selected locations. Below the bar you can see the status representing what you do at this time. Legend you can see below the day tile. Click the day tile to enter the Time Slots screen.

Time Slots

Here you can see what you do at ech time. Zzz means, you take rest - no training.
Click Details button to choose if you want to train, for how much and whom do you prefer.

Provide Training details

Decide with whom you want to play, where, for how much etc.

Time Slots after submitting training offer

Here you can see with icons, that you await an offer. Matching is done every few minutes.

Upper bars are colored if you have chosen them for the training at this time. Bottom bar color represent the status of your offer.
