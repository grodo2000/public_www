Create Account

Follow these steps to create your account quickly and securely.

Step 1

Select your country

Step 2

Choose your User Name. It will be visible to everybody

Step 3

Provide your email address. We will send you an activation link.

Step 4

Choose the sport you are a trainer/player in.

Step 5

Select whether you are a Trainer or a Player

Step 6

Read and accept the Terms of use

Step 7

Verify data and click Create

Logging In

Use your email and password to log in to your account.

Navigation

Use the bottom menu to switch between app sections.

Settings

Manage your account, privacy, and preferences in settings.
