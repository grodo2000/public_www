How to - Create Account

Follow steps below.

Step 1

Select your country

Step 2

Choose your username. This will be visible to everyone, so make sure you don’t include sensitive information.

Step 3

Provide email

Step 4

Select your sport to connect with players / coaches in that category.

Step 5

Choose your role: coach or player.

Step 6

By continuing, you agree to the Terms of Use

Step 7

Create account

After creating your account, you will be redirected to the settings screen.
