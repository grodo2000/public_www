About OnHit Application

The app connects trainers with players through smart matching. Both sides select the clubs where they would like to train or play and define their preferred pricing range (minimum or maximum rate). The system then matches users based on their preferences, such as date, time, location, and price.

Users then select the days and time slots when they are available, as well as the clubs where they would like to train or play. For a single day and time slot, multiple clubs can be selected.

The system then attempts to match a trainer with a player based on their mutual criteria, including availability, location, and pricing preferences. If a suitable match is found, the status of both offers is updated to “Awaiting Booking Confirmation”.

In such a case, all other offers for the same time slot but in different locations are automatically removed, as a user cannot be in multiple places at the same time.

At this stage, the system enables direct communication between users through an in-app chat.

The purpose of the chat is to allow both sides to discuss details, agree on the final price, and decide whether they want to proceed with the training session. If both parties agree, the trainer can confirm the booking by clicking “Confirm Booking”. The status is then updated to “Confirmed”, and the session appears in Upcoming Trainings. Chat remains available after confirmation.

There are different ways of matching trainers with players.

If neither side selects a preferred trainer or preferred player, the matching process is based on standard criteria such as availability, location, and pricing.

If both the trainer and the player select each other as preferred for a given location and time, they are matched directly without additional filtering, regardless of price.

Additionally, if a user selects a Preferred Trainer in their user preferences, they will see that trainer’s offers in their calendar highlighted with pink dots, according to the legend shown at the bottom of each day tile.

When opening the training request screen, users can also select a preferred trainer. By default, this field is pre-filled with the trainer chosen in User Preferences, but it can be changed manually at any time.

Similarly, the price is also pre-set based on the values defined in User Preferences, but users can adjust it freely when creating a request.

How to - Create Account

Follow the steps below.

Step 1

Select your country

Step 2

Choose your username. Your username is visible to everyone. Do not include sensitive or personal information.

Step 3

Provide your email address

Step 4

Select your sport to connect with players and coaches

Step 5

Choose your role: coach or player

Step 6

Read and accept the Terms of Use

Step 7

Create your account

After creating your account, you will be redirected to the preferences screen.

Preferences Overview

Learn how to manage your preferences.

Preferences

Set your training preferences such as location, price, preferred trainer etc.

Select Location

Choose your canton first, then select a club.

Add Location

Click “Add Location” to save your selection.

Multiple Locations

You can add up to 5 training locations.

Preferred Trainer

Selecting a preferred trainer gives them priority over other trainers in the matching process.

After confirming your preferences, the main app screen will appear.

Creating a Training Offer

The idea is to create a Training Offer (if you are a trainer) or a Training Request (if you are a player) and get matched with the other side based on location, price, date, time etc.

Main Screen

Here you see hourly tiles. Above each hour you can see your selected locations. Below each tile you can see the current status. Tap a day to open Time Slots.

Time Slots

Here you see your activity for each time slot. “Zzz” means rest (no training). Tap “Details” to create a training offer.

Training Details

Set your preferences: preferd trainer / player, location, price, and other details.

Awaiting Match

After submitting, your request enters matching mode. The system matches users every few minutes.

Active Schedule

Colored horizontal bars show your selected training times. Status indicators below show the current state of each offer.
